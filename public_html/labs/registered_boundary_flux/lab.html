Instructions

Use this lab as a boundary-flux renderer. It shows a constrained boundary loading, compressing, focusing, releasing, and leaving trace.

  1. Choose a regime from the Regime menu.
  2. Use PREV and NEXT to step through the phase grammar.
  3. Use PAUSE to freeze the renderer for inspection.
  4. Tune Boundary, Interior, Flux, Emission, and Trace.
  5. Drag the renderer to rotate it. Pinch to zoom.

Boundary: this is a visual grammar only. It is not a hydrodynamic solver and not a physical-force simulation.
