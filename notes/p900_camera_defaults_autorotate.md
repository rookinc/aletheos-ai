# P900 Camera Defaults and Auto Rotate

Status: renderer usability refinement

Defaults changed:

    camera distance: 12
    camera yaw: -1.5
    camera pitch: -0.5

Auto rotate:

    Added an Auto rotate checkbox in the Camera card.
    It defaults to on.
    The metrics console reports auto_rotate on/off.

Boundary:

    This is a renderer/view preference change only.
    It does not alter P900 artifact data or simulator overdrive semantics.
